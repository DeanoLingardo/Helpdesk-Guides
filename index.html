Parent level & Child level:
Each job on the system will have a parent level and a child level. The list of jobs you see when logging into plan on are all parent level, clicking on a parent level item (tiny arrow to the left) will show the child level underneath. The Child level is the jobs that are assigned to workers or sent to contractors. Consider the parent level the request and the child level the job.
